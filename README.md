### COFEE SALES PROJECT

**Project Overview:**

**Objective:**

The objective of this project is to analyze coffee sales data to gain insights on the most profitable product categories, identify underperforming products, and provide recommendations to improve sales productivity across all products.

**Scope:**

The analysis focuses on coffee sales data, examining key areas such as busiest day of the week, top-selling products, revenue drivers, hourly sales distribution, sales by location, monthly sales trends, and daily coffee sales.

**Methodology:**

- Data Collection: Gathering logs and company sales records 
- Data Cleansing/Sorting: To remove duplicate values and unwanted variables
- Analysis: Using Pivot Table, and Pivot Chats to extract meaningful information. 
- Visualization Dashboard: was employed for a simple, summary and insightful representation analysis. 

**DATA SOURCE**

This dataset was sourced and downloaded online as XLT & PDF file, for project and academy purposes.

**LIMITATION**

This analysis uses dummy data, findings should be validated with actual sales data

**TOOLS**

• Microsoft Excel: Utilized for preliminary data correction, and initial review of dataset
• Microsoft Excel: Employed for Data Cleaning, in-depth analysis to extract meaningful insights and visualization to provide detailed and interactive reports. 

**Key Findings**

 **(Results)**
- Friday is the busiest day of the week, while Sunday has the least sales.
- Top revenue-generating products: Brewed Chai, Gourmet Brewed, Barista Espresso, Brewed Black, and Hot Chocolate.
- Underperforming products: Green Beans, Green Tea, House Blend Beans, Organic Chocolate, and Clothing.
- June has the highest monthly sales, while February has the lowest.
- Coffee products dominate sales, outselling other products in the store.
- Hell's Kitchen branch generates the most revenue.
- 10 am is the peak hour for sales.

**Recommendations:**

Based on the findings, here are some potential recommendations:

1. Optimize Product Offerings:
    - Promote top-selling products (Brewed Chai, Gourmet Brewed, etc.) through targeted marketing campaigns.
    - Consider discontinuing or rebranding underperforming products (Green Beans, Green Tea, etc.).
2. Improve Sales on Sundays:
    - Run promotions or discounts on Sundays to boost sales.
    - Analyze customer behavior on Sundays to identify opportunities.
3. Capitalize on Peak Hours:
    - Ensure adequate staffing and inventory during peak hours (10 am).
    - Offer limited-time promotions or deals during peak hours.
4. Leverage Hell's Kitchen Branch Success:
    - Analyze factors contributing to Hell's Kitchen's success (location, marketing, etc.).
    - Apply these strategies to other branches.
5. Seasonal Strategies:
    - Develop seasonal promotions or products to capitalize on June's high sales.
    - Offer discounts or bundles during February's low sales period.
6. Coffee Product Focus:
    - Continue to prioritize coffee products, exploring new flavors and brewing methods.
    - Consider expanding coffee-related services (e.g., coffee tastings, brewing classes).

**NOTE:** These recommendations aim to boost sales, optimize product offerings, and leverage successful strategies.
